# Decorators for variables


    p₀ • p₁ • p₂ • …
    p₀ ∙ p₁ ∙ p₂ ∙ ⋯
    p₀ · p₁ · p₂ · ⋯
    p₀ · p₁ · p₂ · ⋯
    p₀ ․ p₁ ․ p₂ ․ ⋯
    p₀ ⋅ p₁ ⋅ p₂ ⋅ …

    p ∼ p ∽ p ⁓ p ¯ p ¨ p
    p ‥ p … p ∶ p ⫶ p ∵ p
    p ‚ p , p ¸ p ͺ p
    p ∘ p ◦ p ° p ◌ p ○ p
    p ◾ p ◽ p ▫ p ▪ p ⨾ p
    p ⋆ p ⋄ p ▿ p ▹ p ‣ p
    p ´ p ″ p ⁗ p ‴ p ‷ p ‶ p ‛ p ‵ p ` p ʹ p ′ p
