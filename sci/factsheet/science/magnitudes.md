# Magnitudes

In base 10 number systems, the magnitudes above 1 million had (some still do) different names across cultures.

UK:
- million   10⁶
- milliard  10
- billion
- billiard
- trillion
- trilliard


$$
\begin{align}
&10^{-12} &=& 0.00000000000        \\
&10^{-9}  &=& 0.00000000        \\
&10^{-6}  &=& 0.000001       \\
&10^{-3}  &=& 0.001       \\
&10^{-2}  &=& 0.01      \\
&10^{-1}  &=& 0.1     \\
&10^0     &=& 1     \\
&10^{1}   &=& 10      \\
&10^{2}   &=& 100       \\
&10^{3}   &=& 1,000       \\
&10^{6}   &=& 1,000,000       \\
&10^{9}   &=& 1,000,000,000       \\
&10^{12}  &=& 1,000,000,000,000
\end{align}
$$
