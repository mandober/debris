# Matrix

- matrix tags: `\begin{matrix}` ... `\end{matrix}`
- huge brackets: `\Bigg[` ... `\Bigg]`

$$
\Bigg[
\begin{matrix}
   a & b & c \\
   m & n & o \\
   x & y & z
\end{matrix}
\Bigg]
        \times
\Bigg[
\begin{matrix}
   \alpha & \beta       & \gamma \\
   \mu    & \epsilon    & \omega \\
   \xi    & \varepsilon & \zeta
\end{matrix}
\Bigg]
$$
