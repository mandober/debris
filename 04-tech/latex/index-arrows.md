## Extensible Arrows

Subscript/superscript above/under arrow don't extend:
- `\overset{u}{\rightarrow}` ✔ mathjax
- `\underoverset{d}{u}{\rightarrow}` (✘ mathjax)

Extensible arrows:
- `\xleftarrow`
  - `\xleftarrow{u}`
  - `\xleftarrow[d]{u}`
- `\xrightarrow`
  - `\xrightarrow{u}`
  - `\xrightarrow[d]{u}`
